I The Invitation

Some books are read once. Others are read every day, for a thousand years.

114 is a reader made for the second kind — an old text, kept close, built for daily attention.

It is made quietly. For readers who return. The reader assumes nothing about who you are or what you believe. It only assumes you would like to read well, and often, and without interruption.

II The Promise

Read. Remember. Return.

Read

Typography considered for daily attention. No banners. No badges. No interruption. The text as the text.

Remember

Bookmarks, passages, small thoughts — kept privately. Synced only if you ask, offline otherwise.

Return

A gentle habit. Streaks without shame. A widget that says nothing of what you read. Only that you read.

Made for those who keep coming back.
