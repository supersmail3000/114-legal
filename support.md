## Common questions

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
iPhone → Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → 114 → Cancel. Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time up to 24 hours before the renewal date.

**How do I restore a purchase on a new device?**
In 114: Settings → Restore Purchases. Use the same Apple ID you purchased with.

**Does 114 work offline?**
Yes. The full Quran is available offline. Daily verse updates require an internet connection.
